League of Legends Champion Recommender

Why?
League of Legends is well-known computer game. It is extremely hard to get better and climb the ranked ladder. I want to make it easier for new players to learn which matchups are good and which champions are currently winning the most games. This application will allow a user to search their champion and see its overall win percentage, number of games played, and the top 3 champions that the searched champion counters or gets countered by. Some examples are Lolcounter.com, op.gg, and leagueofgraphs.com
